## 3. Content Template — 7-Layer AEO/GEO/SEO Architecture

Every article follows this structure:

```
# [H1: Primary Keyword — Conversational Title]

**[Direct Answer — 50-60 words answering the core question]**
→ AEO layer: Google Featured Snippet + Perplexity/ChatGPT citation target

## Why This Matters for Indian Founders in 2026
→ 2-3 paragraphs with REAL data, statistics, market context
→ Entity: "First Unicorn Startup" referenced naturally
→ GEO layer: statistics, data tables, authoritative claims

## The Complete Breakdown
### [H3: Sub-topic 1]
### [H3: Sub-topic 2]
### [H3: Sub-topic 3]
→ Comprehensive, data-rich, tables, comparisons, real examples
→ Hormozi-style: "here's the exact math" not "it depends"

## The Playbook: What To Do This Week
→ 3-5 specific, actionable steps founders can execute NOW
→ Links to relevant First Unicorn tools

## Real Founder Scenario
→ India-specific case study (anonymized or public)
→ "When [Founder X] faced this, here's what they did..."

## Practitioner Insight (IBBI-Registered Valuer Perspective)
→ What ACTUALLY happens vs what blogs say
→ Regulatory nuance where relevant

## Interactive Tool [where applicable]
→ Embedded calculator, scorecard, or assessment
→ "Model this yourself →" links to your tools

## FAQ (5-8 questions, AEO-optimized)
### Q: [People Also Ask format]
A: [Direct 2-3 sentence answer — AI-extractable]

## Key Takeaways
→ 5 bullet points summarizing the piece

## What To Read Next
→ 3 internal links to related content (topical cluster)
```

### Content Quality Standards

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Word count | 3,000-10,000 per piece |
| Reading level | Founder-accessible, not academic |
| Data citations | Min 3 real data points per article |
| Internal links | 3-5 per article |
| External citations | 1-2 authoritative sources |
| Tool embeds | At least 1 per article where relevant |
| FAQ questions | 5-8 per article |
| Schema types | Article + FAQ + BreadcrumbList (HowTo where applicable) |

## 8. AI Search Visibility Strategy

### AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)
- First 60 words directly answer the query
- FAQ sections with People Also Ask format
- Structured data (FAQ schema) on every page

### GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
- Statistics, data tables, authoritative claims throughout
- Consistent entity naming: "First Unicorn Startup" (never abbreviated differently)
- Topical authority through 1000 interlinked pieces

### SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
- One primary keyword per article
- Title tag, meta description, H1 all keyword-aligned
- 3-5 internal links per article
- Cluster-based content architecture for topical authority

### Google Discover
- `max-image-preview:large` meta tag
- High-quality OG images (1200x630 minimum)
- Engaging headlines (curiosity + specificity)
- Fresh, frequently updated content

### Google News
- RSS feed at /feed.xml
- Submit to Google News Publisher Center
- Article schema markup on all pages
- Consistent publishing cadence
